Mini Project Proposal
Assignment Name: Student Dropout Risk Prediction using Student Behaviour Data
1. Problem Statement :
Student dropout is a serious issue in schools and colleges. Many students leave their studies early due to poor attendance, low academic performance, lack of engagement, or personal difficulties. It is important to identify students who are at risk of dropping out at an early stage so that teachers and institutions can provide support.
This project aims to analyze student behaviour data and predict whether a student is at risk of dropping out using machine learning techniques.
2. Dataset Description :
The dataset will include student-related information such as:
Attendance percentage
Marks / academic performance
Assignment completion status
Participation in class activities
Study hours
Behaviour record (if available)
The dataset may be created manually or collected from sample educational datasets available online. These features help identify patterns related to student engagement and performance.
3. Algorithm Used :
This project will use a machine learning classification algorithm such as:
Decision Tree or K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)
These algorithms help classify students into categories like:
High dropout risk
Medium dropout risk
Low dropout risk
The algorithm will analyze student behaviour patterns and predict risk levels based on previous data.
4. Expected Output :
The expected output of this project is:
Identification of students who are at risk of dropping out
Classification of students into different risk levels
Visualization of student performance patterns
A prediction model that helps teachers take early action
This system can help educational institutions improve student success rates by providing timely support and guidance.
